Feedback on 1st version:
1. **Weeks to exam**: this is not ideal. Sometimes candidates do not know exactly when the exam will take place. It may be better to ask: **exam date or exam month?**
2. **When was your last EPSO-style test?** We could allow an approximate date, such as **MM/YYYY**, or even just **YYYY**.
3. The first three questions do not tell me much; I do not clearly understand what value they add. I would rather ask, for candidates who have already taken tests before:
   **Confidence in verbal reasoning** — 1 to 5
   **Confidence in numerical reasoning** — 1 to 5
   **Confidence in abstract reasoning** — 1 to 5
   **Confidence in EU knowledge** — 1 to 5
   In the case of a **specialist competition**:
   **Confidence in the main technical elements of the competition** — ideally with a list of the main items taken from the vacancy notice — 1 to 5.
4. I actually like the idea of including a short test. The questions are not very meaningful at the moment, but I think that can be fixed quite easily.
5. We need to include a way for users to manually enter their results from EU Training or similar platforms, for each category, e.g. **verbal, numerical and abstract reasoning; FRMCQ; EUFTE**.
6. in "today's session" the user needs to see details of what he actually need to do: patterns to focus on, specific knowledge items, etc.
