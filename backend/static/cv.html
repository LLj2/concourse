Your CV

We read your CV once to weight your starting point and flag your fit for the competition you're targeting. Required for specialist competitions, optional otherwise. Adding your LinkedIn profile (as a PDF) sharpens the read.

1Upload your CV

PDF, DOC or DOCX, up to 10 MB. A new upload replaces the previous one.

2Add your LinkedIn profile (optional)

We read your LinkedIn like a second CV. We can't pull it from a link (LinkedIn blocks that), so you export it yourself — about three clicks.

How to get your LinkedIn PDF
  1. Open LinkedIn on a computer and go to your profile.
  2. Click the More button (just under your name).
  3. Choose Save to PDF — your profile downloads as a PDF.
  4. Upload that PDF here. 👇

📱 On the LinkedIn mobile app this option isn't available — do it from a computer.
